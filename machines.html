2. Puddling

Power tiller-mounted cono-puddlers: These are rotary tillers mounted on power tillers that are used to puddle the soil, which involves creating a wet, muddy layer that helps retain water and suppress weeds.

3. Land Leveling

Laser land leveler: This is a high-tech machine that uses lasers to precisely level the land, ensuring uniform water distribution and improving crop growth.

4. Seeding, Planting, and Transplanting

Power tiller and tractor-drawn seed drills: These are machines that can sow seeds in rows at a precise depth and spacing.

Rice transplanter: This machine is used to transplant seedlings that have been grown in a nursery into the paddy field.

5. Weeding

Power tiller-operated weeders: These are machines that use rotating blades to cut weeds between the rice plants.

6. Fertilizer Application

Deep placement applicators: These are machines that inject fertilizer into the soil at a specific depth, which can improve fertilizer efficiency and reduce waste.

7. Plant Protection

Power sprayers: These are machines that use a pump to apply herbicides, pesticides, and fungicides to crops.

8. Harvesting

Combine harvester: This is a machine that cuts, threshes, and cleans rice in one pass. While not yet widespread in eastern India, it is becoming increasingly common.

9. Threshing

Power-operated axial flow threshers: These are machines that use a rotating drum to thresh rice, separating the grains from the straw.

10. Straw Management Machinery

Straw chopper-cum-spreader: This machine chops up rice straw and spreads it evenly over the field, where it can decompose and improve soil fertility.

Happy seeder: This is a machine that can sow wheat seeds directly into standing rice straw, eliminating the need for tillage and reducing labor costs.
